For better, open government

Civil society groups around the world are using the Open Government Partnership process to achieve real gains in transparency, participation and accountability.

Canada is already preparing its third Action Plan on Open Government as part of this process.

We are working to establish a permanent dialogue mechanism with the Government of Canada to coordinate civil society input and make sure our voices are heard.

To join the network as a civil society organization or an individual, read our letter of intent and contact join@opengovdialogue.ca to sign the letter.
